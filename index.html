BIOGRAPHY

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, better known
as APJ Abdul Kalam, was an illustrious scientist turned
statesman who served as the 11th President of India
from 2002 to 2007.Dr. A P J Abdul Kalam was born to a
poor Tamil Muslim family. He lived with his
family in the temple city of Tamilnadu, Rameswaram.
He ias also called Missile Man of India
